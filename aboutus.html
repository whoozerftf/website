About Us

We’re not for everyone.

We’re for the bold, the fearless, the ones who move first.

Whoozer was born on the streets—
not in fancy studios, not from trends.

We come from long nights, loud sounds, and real anger.

We’ve been standing strong in Tambun since December 25, 2024.

Our name?

“Whooz” — fast, unpredictable.
“-er” — the doers, the boundary breakers.

We’re not spectators. We’re the ones in motion.
Moving faster than fear.
Running, not waiting.
Breaking rules, not asking.

You don’t need permission to join.
You just need one thing:

The courage to be faster.

Whoozer.
Faster Than Fear.
