Privacy Policy – Whoozer

Last updated: May 2025

At Whoozer, protecting your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and payment details when you make a purchase or subscribe. We also collect data automatically through cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us:

  • Process orders and payments
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send you marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Analyze site traffic and usage
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance website functionality and performance. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect website usability.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party service providers to operate our business, including:

  • Shopify: Our e-commerce platform that processes orders and securely stores your transaction data. Shopify’s privacy practices can be found here: Shopify Privacy Policy.
  • Xendit: Our payment gateway provider responsible for securely processing your payment information. Learn more about their privacy practices here: Xendit Privacy Policy.

We only share your personal data with these providers as necessary to provide our services.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (for EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data based on your consent, contractual necessity, or to comply with legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. You can exercise these rights by contacting us at whoozerftf@gmail.com.

7. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with trusted partners essential to our operations and implement technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are not intended for individuals under 16 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review it periodically for any changes.
